(About)

A cybersecurity company with a builder's obsession.

PALANIT is a cybersecurity company. We don't just consult — we build, launch, and grow security products and brands of our own. Security can't be an afterthought, so we ship it from line one.

Founded by an information-security specialist turned builder, PALANIT turns deep offensive-security expertise into products that people and companies can actually trust. We think like attackers and build like engineers.

(Founder)

Built by a security obsessive, not a committee.

PALANIT was founded by an information-security specialist and builder — someone who spent years on the offensive side, finding how systems break, then decided to build the products that fix them.
