(About)

A venture studio with a builder's obsession.

PALANIT is a venture studio. We don't just consult — we build, launch, and grow our own digital products and brands, from marketplaces and platforms to security tools.

Our edge is a security-first mindset: founded by an information-security specialist, we build products that are fast, useful, and hard to break. We think like attackers and ship like a startup.

(Founder)

PALANIT was founded by an information-security specialist and builder — someone who spent years finding how systems break, then turned that into a studio that ships real products people use every day.
